# govunity_connect

*Scheme Database*: The app hosts a comprehensive database of government schemes spanning multiple sectors such as agriculture, education, healthcare, finance, employment, and more. Each scheme is categorized and detailed information is provided including eligibility criteria, benefits, application process, required documents, and deadlines.

*Scheme Details*: Upon selecting a scheme, users can view detailed information including objectives, benefits, eligibility criteria, application process, documents required, and contact information of relevant authorities or departments.

*Language Support*: The app supports multiple languages to cater to users from diverse linguistic backgrounds across India. It ensures that information about government schemes is accessible and understandable to a wide audience.
